Cymusic
Client

MDS

Service

Visual design

Category

Technology, UX

The Cymusic project was about stepping out of my comfort zone and trying something I had never done before. The project aimed to combine music and cymatics to make music more accessible. "Cymatics" refers to the study of visible sound and vibration—essentially, when certain frequencies are played, specific patterns are formed. I recreated this phenomenon using the mathematical formulas of cymatic patterns and TouchDesigner.
