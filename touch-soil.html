With food costs climbing and fruit and vegetable prices up by 23% over the past year, it’s becoming harder for university students to make nutritious meals without breaking the bank. That’s where Touch Soil steps in. Touch Soil is a practical, step-by-step guide to growing your own produce, empowering you to add fresh, homegrown ingredients to your meals without the high grocery bills. Whether you’re new to gardening or just looking to save on groceries, Touch Soil makes it easy to bring healthy, homegrown food to your plate.
